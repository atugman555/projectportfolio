Get to Know Me!

Hello there! I’m Anthony Tugman, an Intelligent Systems Engineering student at Indiana University Bloomington. After completing my B.S. in Intelligent Systems Engineering – Cyber Physical Systems in 2020, I continued my studies and will complete my M.S in Intelligent Systems Engineering – Cyber Physical Systems in May 2021. My academic and professional endeavors have developed both the skill set and professional background necessary to take a deep dive into developing cutting edge products upon graduation. With an interest in product development, my focus has revolved around rapid prototyping, hardware/software integration, PCB layout/design, and data visualization. I consider myself a “forever student”, eager to both build on my academic foundations in the transition to a professional career as well as staying in tune with the latest developments in this discipline. I am a maker, a problem solver, and a creator; if a solution doesn’t exist or the current solution can be improved I’m the first to take a try at it.

In my personal time I am working to expand my knowledge into the areas of blockchain, cybersecurity, and app development. In true entrepreneurial spirit I am frequently collaborating with others on new ideas, technologies, and ways to capitalize on my experience and knowledge.
